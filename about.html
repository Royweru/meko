About Us

Cooking with solar energy presents a remarkable fusion of sustainability and culinary excellence, and at its forefront are solar-powered cookers, often affectionately called "mekos." These innovative devices tap into the boundless energy of the sun to create a cleaner and renewable alternative to traditional cooking methods. Mekos are ingeniously designed, featuring reflective surfaces such as mirrors or metallic panels that harness and concentrate sunlight onto cooking vessels or surfaces. This concentrated solar energy achieves swift temperature elevation, enabling a diverse array of cooking styles, from grilling to baking, boiling, and steaming. Whether crafting savory vegetable dishes, tender meats, or artisanal bread, mekos using solar energy introduce a greener and more energy-efficient dimension to gastronomic artistry. Beyond the gastronomic realm, cooking with mekos is a conscious choice that leaves a minimal carbon footprint, fostering sustainability by reducing reliance on fossil fuels or electricity. Additionally, this solar-powered culinary approach is a perfect companion for outdoor cooking adventures, camping escapades, or off-grid living, offering culinary freedom without compromising environmental ethics. Discover the remarkable potential of harnessing the sun's power with mekos and savor the eco-conscious path to preparing your favorite meals. It's a culinary revolution that not only tantalizes taste buds but also respects our planet.
